## Notation & Definition

$D$: open, closed, bounded, connected, compact에 대한 정의는 생략. (compact = closed + bounded)

$\partial D$: boundary of $D$

$\overline{D}$: closure of $D = D \cup \partial D$

$B_r(x) = B(x, r) = \{ y \in \mathbb{R}^n \Big | y - x | \le r \}$

$B_r(x)^\circ = B^\circ(x, r) = y \in \mathbb{R}^n |y - x| < r$

$\partial B(x, r) = \partial B_\circ (x, r) = y \in \mathbb{R}^n |y - x| = r$

$\mathrm{supp} \ f$: Support of $f = \overline{ \{ x f(x) \neq 0 \} }$

$x \in \mathbb{R}^n \to x = (x_1, \cdots, x_n)$, $|x| = \sqrt{x_1^2 + \cdots + x_n^2}$
 
$A \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n$. $|A|$: Lebergue measure of $A$ (size of $A$)

example: 
for $n = 1$, $A = [1, 2] \to |A| = 1$
for $n = 2$, $A = B(x, r) \to |A| = \pi r^2$

$\Delta$: Laplacian. 두 번 미분의 합.

$u: D \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}$일 때

$n = 1$: $u = u(x)$, $\Delta u = u''$

$n = 2$: $u = u(x_1, x_2)$, $\Delta u = \partial_1^2 u + \partial_2^2 u$

$n = 3$: $u = u(x_1, x_2, x_3)$, $\Delta u = \partial_1^2 u + \partial_1^2 u + \partial_1^3 u$

## Function Space

$D \in \mathbb{R}^n$일 때

$C (D) = \{ f: D \to \mathbb{R} \Big f : \text{ continuous } \}$

$C^1 (D) = \{ f: D \to \mathbb{R} \Big f : \text{ differentiable, } \partial_1 f, \cdots, \partial_n f : \text{ continuous } \}$

$C^2 (D) = \{ f: D \to \mathbb{R} \Big f : \text{ differentiable twice, } 
\partial_1\partial_1 f, \partial_1\partial_2 f, \cdots, \partial_{n}\partial_{n-1} f, \partial_n\partial_n f : \text{ continuous } \}$

example:

$$f(x) = \begin{cases}
    x^{\frac{3}{2}} \sin \frac{1}{x} \quad & x \neq 0 \\
    0 & x = 0
\end{cases}$$

is differentiable at $x = 0$ but $f'$ is not continuous at $x = 0$. i.e, $f \in C(\mathbb{R})$, $f \notin C^1(\mathbb{R})$

$C^\infty (D) = \bigcap_{n=0}^\infty C^n (D)$: infinitely differentiable.

## Harmonic Function

Def.

$u: D \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}$일 때, 

$u$ is harmonic in $D$ if $\Delta u(x) = 0$ for all $x \in D$.

$$\partial_1^2 u(x)  + \cdots + \partial_n^2 u(x) = 0$$ for all $x \in D$.
